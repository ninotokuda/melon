Organize

Whether it is the movie night with your friends, your mothers birthday party or an important meeting with your client. Reminder+ allows you list up all the tasks and share with all involved persons.

Get notified

As soon someone shares a list with you or completes a task, you receive a notification. So you are up to speed with all your tasks.

Keep track

You can easily keep track of all your tasks. Change and complete tasks on the flight, all shared and delegated lists will be synchronized automaticly.
